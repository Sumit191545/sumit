About COVID-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discoverded coronavirus.This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub.

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or fiexed elbow when you cough or sneeze.

Avoid close contact(1 meter or three feet) with people who are unwell.

Stay home and self-isolated from others in the household if you feel unwell.

Stay informed by watching news and follow the recommended practices.

If you have fever, cough and difficulty breathing seek medical care early.
